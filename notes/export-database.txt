
To export from database 'nyc1':

  sudo su postgres
  /opt/pg9/bin/pg_dump -n hard -U postgres nyc1 > /var/tmp/pgdump-hard-YYYYMMDD.sql
  gzip -9 /var/tmp/pgdump-hard-YYYYMMDD.sql

To export into another database 'nyc2' (on the same host):

  sudo su postgres
  gunzip /var/tmp/pgdump-hard-YYYYMMDD.sql.gz
  /opt/pg9/bin/psql -U postgres -d nyc2 -f /var/tmp/pgdump-hard-YYYYMMDD.sql

However to import to a fresh new host:

  /opt/pg9/bin/psql -U postgres -d nyc2 -f sql/create-roles.sql
